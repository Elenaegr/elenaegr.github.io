A little introduction of myself

My name is Elena, I am 27 years old and I am from Spain. I studied a Bachelor's Degree in Telecommunication engineering at the University of Granada and afterwards I worked for almost 3 years mainly in the field of Sotfware Development and Operations. However, I would love to focus my future career in the Physical Interaction Design subject area and that's why I am currently pursuing the Interactive Media Technology Master's at KTH Royal Institue of Technology in Stockholm, Sweden. I am passionate about learning new things every day and understanding how everything works at a low level. I feel ready to embrace the technologies of the future on a rational and sustainable way.
